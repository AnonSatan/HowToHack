The Hacker's 10 Commandments

  1. Be proactively paranoid, it doesn't work retroactively.
  2. Keep personal activities and hacking activities completely separate.
  3. Keep your personal environment contraband free.
  4. Trust no one.
  5. Never give anyone power over you.
  6. Never reveal your plans.
  7. Never reveal your operational details.
  8. Never confuse recreation and hacking.
  9. Never operate from your home IP.
  10. Never talk to the police.

Appendix

  • No one is going to go to jail for you.
  • If you have to say, "don't tell anyone", don't FUCKING tell them!
  • If you have to talk to someone, talk to your lawyer.
  • Don't hang out in hacker channels on IRC to make friends.

Personas (nicks, handles, aliases, covers)

Avoid Contamination

  • Danger to personas is cross-contamination.
  • Contact between personas contaminates both of them.
  • Keep personas isolated from each other.
  • ProTip: Don't use your real first name as your username.

Layer your Defenses.

  • A persona is most effective at the end.
  • Prepare multiple personas.
  • Prepare to replace personas at anytime.

Fail safe and secure.

  • Your primary cover persona is your first identity.
  • Have a back stop persona ready.
  • Use secondary cover personas as needed.
  • Tor all the things!

VPN vs. TOR

  • VPNs provide privacy (data).
  • TOR provides anonymity (you).
  • Confuse the two at your own peril!

No logs, no crime.

Pitfalls

Personal info is profiling info

Location revealing information.

  • Weather
  • Time
  • Political events

Misdirect. LIE!

  • Do not discuss personal information, e.g. where you are from.
  • Do not include personal information in you online identity, e.g. nick, username, etc.
  • Do not mention your physical traits, e.g. gender, tatoos, piercings, or physical capacities.
  • Do not mention your profession, hobbies or involvement in activist groups.
  • Do not post information to the regular internet while you are anonymous in IRC.
  • Do not use Twitter or Facebook.
  • Do not post links to Facebook images. The image contains a personal ID.

Anti-Location Profiling

  • Do not keep regular hours / habits (This can reveal your timezone, geographic locale).
  • Do not use special characters on you keyboard unique to your language.

Practice safety or perish alone.
